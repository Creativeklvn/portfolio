About

Discover My Story

Kelvin Uneze is a visual artist, graphic designer, front-end developer and UI/UX designer born in Lagos, Nigeria. He had his National and Higher Diploma (HND) in Painting at Auchi Polytechnic, Edo State, Nigeria. Skilled in digital art, graphic design, web development, UI/UX front-end development and multimedia design. His passion for design and cooperation with people drives him to constantly search for new inspirations and challenges, he is passionate about leveraging artistic and design talents in contributing to innovative, visually stunning projects in a dynamic creative environment by crafting engaging visuals that enhance user experience and brand perception.
