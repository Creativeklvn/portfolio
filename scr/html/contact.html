About

Discover My Story

Kelvin Uneze is a creative multimedia artist, graphic designer and UI/UX designer born in Lagos, Nigeria. He had his National and Higher Diploma (HND) in Painting and General Art at Auchi Polytechnic, Edo State, Nigeria. He had his first degree in Fine and Applied Arts with specialization in painting at Auchi Polytechnic, Edo State, Nigeria. He is Skilled in digital art, fine art and multimedia design, with experience at Twins Photos Digital Studio where he worked as a photographer and graphic designer, KullStore company as a graphic designer and Data Point computer Institute as a tutor in graphic design. In 2024, Kelvin exhibited in the 5th Edition Miniature Art Fair 2024 by Ogirikan Art Gallery and Iwalewa Gallery of Art. He was featured among the contestants for "Unleash your Creativity" which was organized by Plein Air Lagos. Developed and enhanced visual content that aligns with brand identity and marketing objectives. He developed his skills consistently in designs and visual art and strive to create designs that are not only visually appealing but also communicate a message effectively. He is proficiency in Figma, adobe photoshop, coreldraw, illustration, framer and mircrosoft. His passion for design and cooperation with people drives him to constantly search for new inspirations and challenges, he is passionate about leveraging artistic and design talents in contributing to innovative, visually stunning projects in a dynamic creative environment by crafting engaging visuals that enhance user experience and brand perception.
